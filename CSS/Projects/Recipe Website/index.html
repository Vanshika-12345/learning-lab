Shahi Paneer

Ingredients

  • Paneer (cottage cheese) – cubed
  • Onions – chopped
  • Tomatoes – chopped or pureed
  • Cashew nuts
  • Fresh cream
  • Milk (optional for richness)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies – chopped
  • Butter or ghee
  • Oil
  • Bay leaf
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Green cardamom
  • Cloves
  • Cumin seeds
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt – to taste
  • Kasuri methi (dry fenugreek leaves)
  • Coriander leaves – for garnish

Recipe

  1. Soak cashew nuts in warm water for 15–20 minutes, then blend into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a pan. Add bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and cumin seeds. Sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell goes away.
  5. Add chopped or pureed tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the cashew paste and cook for a few minutes on medium heat.
  8. Pour in milk or water to adjust the gravy consistency and bring to a boil.
  9. Add paneer cubes and let it simmer for 5–7 minutes.
  10. Stir in fresh cream and crushed kasuri methi. Mix gently.
  11. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for 1–2 more minutes.
  12. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya (mawa) – grated
  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Baking soda – a pinch
  • Milk – for kneading
  • Ghee or oil – for deep frying
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom pods
  • Rose water (optional)
  • Saffron strands (optional)
  • Pistachios – for garnish

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, all-purpose flour, and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Gradually add milk and knead into a soft, smooth dough.
  3. Cover and let it rest for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, make sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and cardamom pods until slightly sticky. Add rose water and saffron if using.
  5. Divide dough into small balls without cracks.
  6. Heat ghee or oil on low-medium flame and deep fry balls until golden brown.
  7. Soak fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 1 hour.
  8. Garnish with pistachios and serve warm.

Hakka Noodles

Ingredients

  • Hakka noodles
  • Capsicum – sliced
  • Carrot – julienned
  • Cabbage – shredded
  • Spring onions – chopped
  • Garlic – minced
  • Ginger – julienned
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Black pepper
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil – preferably sesame or refined

Recipe

  1. Boil noodles as per instructions. Drain and toss with little oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Add garlic and ginger. Sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry on high flame until slightly cooked but still crunchy.
  4. Add boiled noodles and toss well.
  5. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
